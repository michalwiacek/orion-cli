# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

1. Being respectful and constructive.
2. Assuming good intent.
3. Accepting and giving feedback gracefully.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

1. Harassment or personal attacks.
2. Discrimination of any kind.
3. Trolling, insults, or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributors that violate this policy.
